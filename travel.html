  1. Rishikesh
    2.  location

    Rishikesh is a spiritual town located in the foothills of the Himalayas in northern India, where the sacred Ganges River flows down from the mountains. It is widely known as the "Yoga Capital of the World", attracting seekers of yoga, meditation, and spiritual enlightenment from across the globe. The city gained international fame when The Beatles visited the ashram of Maharishi Mahesh Yogi in 1968.
